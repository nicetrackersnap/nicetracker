## ✨ What you get

- 🃏 **Deck tracker** — see exactly which cards are still in your deck and what you've drawn, live, as you play.
- 👀 **Opponent tracker** — every card you've seen the opponent play, plus a heads-up when you've **faced this opponent before** (and how it went).
- 📊 **Deck stats bar** — win/loss/tie record, net cubes, and average cubes per game / win / loss, scoped to the mode you're in (Ranked, Conquest, events).
- 📈 **Stats dashboard** — full match history with deck, card, and opponent analytics in your browser.
- 🐾 **A pixel pet** — lives on your overlay, grows up, reacts to big moments (8-cube wins, streaks), and can be fed and dressed up. Purely for fun.
- 📺 **Built for streaming** — every overlay pane doubles as an OBS browser source, and there's a stream-friendly mode.
- 💬 **Twitch chat reactions** *(optional)* — your pet reacts to subs, raids, and chatters.
- 📱 **Mobile match recording** — log games you play on your phone, too.
- 🔄 **Auto-updates** — new versions install themselves in the background.

## 🚀 Get started

1. **[Download the installer](https://github.com/nicetrackersnap/nicetracker/releases/latest/download/NiceTracker-win-Setup.exe)** and run it. It installs to your user folder — no admin prompt.
2. Launch **Nice Tracker**, then open **Marvel Snap**. The overlay attaches on its own.
3. That's it — trackers appear in-game, and the stats dashboard lives at **http://localhost:61700/stats**.

> **First launch may show a Windows SmartScreen prompt** (the app isn't code-signed yet) — click **More info → Run anyway**. It's safe; the warning just means the publisher isn't yet verified with Microsoft.

Prefer no installer? Grab the **[portable .zip](https://github.com/nicetrackersnap/nicetracker/releases/latest/download/NiceTracker-win-Portable.zip)**.
